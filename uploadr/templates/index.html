Goal of Deliverable

-Client is looking for untapped markets for lead generation in commercial real estate brokerage space. 
-Client currently goes onto a states corporation division website (ex: https://ecorp.sos.ga.gov/ ) . First, he types in address of a potential commercial real estate property (picture #1). Next, he goes to filing history (picture #3). Next, he downloads each PDF associated with the corporation (very time consuming). Lastly, he goes into each PDF to find 4 main pieces of information (LLC name, address of property, president of LLC, and president's contact information) 
-This is time consuming for the client.
-The client has two main goals for deliverable which we will call "base" case and "advanced" case.

Base - Client would like a way to scrape all PDFs associated with corporations that are of interest to him (either a complete scrape of the website or have client provide the corporations that are of interest)
Advanced - After scraping PDFs off the website, he would like a spread sheet that contains the 4 main pieces of information (bolded above) so that he can have easily accessible data for lead generation.
